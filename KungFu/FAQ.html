Frequently Asked Questions

How much are membership fees?
The membership fee for a semester is $30 and for the year it will be $35 and will include two white t-shirts.
Do i require prior experience or knowledge of martial arts to join?
No previous experience or knowledge is required. All students and community members of any level are welcomed to join.
Do I need any uniform?
We do not enforce any uniform policy onto our members, but we suggest that members wear something that is light and easy to move in. We also encourage our club members to wear the club t-shirt to practice.
I am from the Kung Fu/Tai Chi class can I come to club practice?
Yes. Anyone from either the Kung Fu/Tai Chi or Jian Shu class are welcome to stop by to the club practice. We will offer help to any student of those three courses with class materials or forms learned during the semester.
Can I come watch and ask questions during one of your club practices?
Yes. Any student is welcome to come to our club practices. Our practices are open to the public and you are welcome to ask any questions you have.
